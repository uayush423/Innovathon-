UTI unifies India’s transport ecosystem — connecting drivers, owners, senders, and receivers on a single, efficient platform.

AI-powered matching connects senders with the right drivers instantly.

Optimized Routing

Minimize fuel costs and delivery times with intelligent route planning.

Real-Time Tracking

Monitor shipment progress with live location updates.

Price Estimation

Transparent cost calculation based on distance and load type.

Voice Assistance

Multilingual support for easy communication between drivers and receivers.

Digital Payments

Secure, UPI-based transactions with automated record-keeping.

Driver Verification

Integrated KYC ensures safety and accountability within the network.

Role-Based Access

Secure portals tailored for Drivers, Truck Owners, Senders, and Receivers.

Our Vision

To build a unified digital infrastructure that powers logistics efficiency, transparency, and data-driven decision-making for the entire nation. UTI bridges the gap between transport operators and government systems for a truly connected India.
